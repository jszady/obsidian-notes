- Establishments 
	- Are permanent
	- Covered in  [[Art 49 TFEU]]
		- if you wanna move establishments into another country you need to go by their leagal system 
- Services Vs Establishment 
	- Establishments should take priority 
	- There must be a cross border element otherwise EU law cannot apply [[Gustafsson]]
- Exceptions 
	- Services 
		- [[Art 51 TFEU]]
			- states that the right of establishment does not apply to ‘activities… connected, even occasionally, with the exercise of official authority'
- [[Art 52 TFEU]]
	- The provisions of this Chapter and measures taken in pursuance thereof shall not prejudice the applicability of provisions laid down by law, regulation or administrative action providing for special treatment for foreign nationals on grounds of
		- public policy,
		- public security
		- or public health.
- Rule of Reason 
	- Mandatory requirements 
		- A restriction on free movement of services can  be justified if ‘for the general good’. However, this must be proportional.
			- It must be for the general good 
				- it must be undiscriminatory
- Examples of how this works in practice 
	- Healthcare 
	- Gambeling 
	- Self-employed
