## Key Points

- What is the point of integration ?
	- to tie countries together for the purpose so that everyone economically can rely on each other
- What are the levels of integration ?
	- Free Trade Area
		- Eliminate tariffs among countries within the EU
		- Countries can still have their own trade policies with nonmembers
		- Example: EFTA
	- Custom Union
		- The same common external tariff for imports outside the EU
		- Example: if EU says that 10% tariffs on Chinese steel every member must apply that 10% 
	- Common Market 
		- Includes customs union and free movement of goods services people and capital
	- Economic Union 
		- adds shared fiscal policy taxation and social welfare rules
	- Political Union 
		- Full unification under one government
- The modern UE today 
	- 27 member states 
	- built on 2 key points 
		- Single Market 
			- removes legal barriers 
		- Customs Market 
			- removes fiscal barriers 
		- Together they allow the free movement of 
			- Goods
			- persons
			- Services
			- Capital 
- The EU as a "Sui Generis" entity 
	- means "of it's own kind" [[Article 4 TEU]] (2)
	- it combines features of states
- Vertical Vs Horizontal Structure 
	- Vertical 
		- EU having authority over member states 
	- Horizontal 
		- Member states acting as equals 
	- The Eu tries to be a community of equals but some hierarchy is needed to enforce laws 
- Obligation of Member states 
	- [[Article 2 TEU]] 
		- EU built on values of dignity democracy rule of law and human rights 
	- [[Article 7 TEU]]
		- Allows EU to suspend a Member State's rights if it breaks those values 
- EU is not the Council of Europe
	- Council of Europe (1949) is separate from the EU 
	- Repsonsible for the European Convverions on Human Rights (ECHR)
	- Cases go to the European Court of Human Rights (Strasbourg)
	- All Eu members are also members of the Council of Europe but they are different bodies ???
