## Key Points
- Doctrinal Approach 
	- this is looking at case law and statues 
	- rules, principles of the area of law 
- Philosophical Approach 
	- this studies law philosophically or theoretically 
	- looking at things like what is law 
	- or testing a theory 
	- Ex: applying vulnerability theory into the law (law and society)
- Historical Approach 
	- study of history of law 
	- how we got to the law today / how its come to be 
	- how people work with the law in the **past**
	- looking at larger influences of the law 
- Comparative Approach 
	- study of law in different countries in different countries 
- Socio-legal Approach
	- its the law's relationship in society
- Empirical Approach 
	- gaining knowledge from experimentations (questions, surveys)
