# Facts

- Mrs. Mountford rented rooms from Mr. Street under a written “licence,” but she lived there exclusively.
## Issue

- Was it a lease or a licence?
## Decision

- It was a **lease**.
## Reasoning

- A lease exists where there is **exclusive possession**, **for a term**, and **at a rent**.
- Labels (“licence” vs “lease”) don’t matter; substance does.
## Ratio

- Exclusive possession for a term at rent = **lease**, even if called a “licence.”
