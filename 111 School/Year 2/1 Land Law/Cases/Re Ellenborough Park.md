# Facts

- Homeowners around a park claimed a right to use it. The owner argued no valid easement existed.
## Issue

- What are the requirements for a valid easement?
## Decision

- The right to use the park was a valid **easement**.
## Reasoning

Set out four requirements for an easement:

1. There must be a **dominant** and **servient** tenement.
2. The right must **accommodate** the dominant land.
3. The owners must be **different people**.
4. The right must be **capable of forming the subject matter of a grant.**

## Ratio
- Established the **four-part test** for valid easements.
