# Law of Property Act 1925

## Key sections
- **s1(1)(a)-(b):** Lists legal estates — fee simple and term of years absolute.
- **s34(2):** Max four legal co-owners.
- **s52:** Transfers of land must be by deed.
- **s53:** Trusts must be in writing; implied trusts exempt.
- **s87:** Mortgages create the same rights as fee simple ownership.
- **s205:** Defines “land” to include physical land, buildings, and rights over land.

## Purpose / context
- Modernised English property law and consolidated ownership and transfer rules.
## Interpretation issues
- Distinction between legal and equitable ownership.
- Section 205 used for defining the extent of “land.
- Section 53 crucial in trust and equitable ownership cases.
